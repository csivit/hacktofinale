The Management Hack
Computer Society of India has been given the prestigious opportunity to organize a media and development hackfest – A week long technical fest starting with workshops and ending with an overnight hackathon. A hackathon is an event in which computer programmers and others involved software development and hardware development, including graphic designers, interface designers and project managers, collaborate intensively on software projects. You have been given the Event Coordinator responsibility.
Submit an event proposal answering the following questions [a-e] and then the situational questions [f-j]:

a. Give an extremely detailed itinerary of the weeklong hackfest including time-wise split up, workshop topics, overnight hackathon schedule, etc.
b. You have been given Rs. 1,00,000 funding for this hackathon. Give us a breakdown of how and where the money will be spent, every rupee accounted for. If you do not know the price of any material, you can use google to check up prices, or give us an estimate.
c. Give us 5 marketing strategies to sell the event such that every student in every year in the university will want to attend the event irrespective of their stream.
d. Mention 7 project topics (tracks) on which the participants can make their project. Eg: Health, Music, etc.
e. You have a workforce of 25 people. Distribute the duties you think are relevant for this event.
f. Consider a situation where the venue has been changed at the last minute from an AC Hall with a capacity of 700 people to a Non-AC Hall with a capacity of 500 people, but you have already publicized the event with big gusto and gathered 700 registrations for the event. The venue change has to be intimated to 700 people at the very last minute, how will you handle the situation?
g. Consider a situation where one of the sponsors decide that they want to visit the University during the period of the Hackathon, they have a list of demands like:
- Putting up banners of their company all around campus.
- Desks at all the academic buildings.
- A mention on the VIT Website
- More engagement with students.
How will you cater to their needs considering that, it’s a CSI only event and publicity only at the venue is allowed and not all over campus?
h. Consider a situation where the food that was organized for lunch at 2pm has not arrived and the participants are getting restless, how do you plan on keeping the participants calm and keep them engaged.
i. Consider a situation where due to certain issues, an overnight event cannot be organized, so the chapter has been given an ultimatum – No Event or Event with time and other restrictions – how would you proceed with the event?
j. Consider a situation where your entire folder of permission documents have been lost. All permissions ranging from venue permission, to sponsorship bonds, to overnight permissions were included in that folder. What all steps will you take to prevent such a situation from happening and how will you handle it?

Hardware
Semiconductor devices manufacturing involves a lot of different processes. One such process is post assembly testing. This process occurs after addition of an enclosure and the wire bonding step of the IC. The machine used for post assembly testing is called a Pick & Place Handler or PPH. This PPH machine gets its input as a tray of ICs and sorts the output into 2 trays. Tray 1 consists of faulty ICs and Tray 2 consists of good ICs. Before the testing process, the PPH machine blows air at the IC for 0.5 seconds from the right side and for 0.3 seconds from the left. The circuit that controls this blower implements use of 2 solenoids.
Create the circuit diagram for this circuit using:
1. An Arduino Pro Mini chip
2. 2 Solenoids
3. 1 ULN2003 Integrated Circuit
4. 1 PM R24 Sensor
5. 1 2.2k resistor
6. 1 PC817 Integrated Circuit
7. 1 16x2 pin LCD Module (To display time)
